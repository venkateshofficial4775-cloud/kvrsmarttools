About KVR Smart Tools

The vision behind the ultimate Android utility suite.

Our Mission

At KVR Smart Tools, our mission is straightforward: to declutter your smartphone by combining over 100 essential daily utilities into one seamless, fast, and beautifully designed application.

We grew tired of downloading separate apps for QR scanning, PDF merging, network testing, and unit conversion—apps that were often bloated with advertisements, paywalls, and hidden tracking scripts. So, we built the all-in-one solution.

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

KVR Smart Developer ("we", "our", or "us") is deeply committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how KVR Smart Tools handles data. Because our application is designed to be highly secure and offline-first, our data collection is strictly minimal to non-existent.

2. Data Collection & Usage

KVR Smart Tools operates locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, distribute, sell, or store your personal data on external servers. Files processed via PDF tools, text converted, passwords generated, and calculations made remain entirely on your smartphone's local storage or RAM during runtime.

3. Permissions Required

To function correctly, KVR Smart Tools may request certain device permissions. These are used strictly locally for the specific tool you are using:

  • Camera: Required only for the QR & Barcode Scanner tools. Images are processed locally and not saved.
  • Storage / Media: Required to save generated PDFs, export text files, or read files you select for processing.
  • Network/Internet: Required strictly for specific tools (e.g., IP Lookup, Currency Converter fetching exchange rates).

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

KVR Smart Tools contains absolutely no third-party advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs or tracking SDKs that share your behavioral data with third parties.

5. Contact Us
