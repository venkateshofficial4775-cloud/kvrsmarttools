Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

KVR Smart Developer ("we", "our", or "us") is deeply committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how KVR Smart Tools handles data. Because our application is designed to be highly secure and offline-first, our data collection is strictly minimal to non-existent.

2. Data Collection & Usage

KVR Smart Tools operates locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, distribute, sell, or store your personal data on external servers. Files processed via PDF tools, text converted, passwords generated, and calculations made remain entirely on your smartphone's local storage or RAM during runtime.

3. Permissions Required

To function correctly, KVR Smart Tools may request certain device permissions. These are used strictly locally for the specific tool you are using:

  • Camera: Required only for the QR & Barcode Scanner tools. Images are processed locally and not saved.
  • Storage / Media: Required to save generated PDFs, export text files, or read files you select for processing.
  • Network/Internet: Required strictly for specific tools (e.g., IP Lookup, Currency Converter fetching exchange rates).

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

KVR Smart Tools contains absolutely no third-party advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs (like AdMob) or tracking SDKs that share your behavioral data with third parties.

5. Children's Privacy

Our app does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. The app is safe for all ages.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy occasionally. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. Changes are effective immediately after they are posted on this page.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us at: venkatesh.official4775@gmail.com.

Data Safety (Google Play)

This section provides the necessary transparency required for Google Play's Data Safety section.

Data Collection and Security

Here is a detailed breakdown of how KVR Smart Tools handles user data:

  • No Data Shared with Third Parties: The developer does not share user data with other companies or organizations.
  • No Data Collected: The developer does not collect user data. All processing (e.g., PDF merging, password generation) occurs locally on the device silicon.
  • Data is Encrypted in Transit: For the few tools that require an internet connection (like API calls for real-time currency conversion), all data is transferred over a secure HTTPs connection.
  • No Account Required: You do not need to create an account, provide an email, or log in to use any feature of this app.

Device Permissions Justification

When the app requests permissions, it is solely to enable local features:

  • Camera: Used exclusively in real-time by the Barcode/QR tools. No visual data is captured or saved unless explicitly commanded by the user to save a scan result locally.
  • Read/Write External Storage: Necessary for the app to access documents you wish to edit (e.g., PDF tools) and to save the processed output back to your local files.
