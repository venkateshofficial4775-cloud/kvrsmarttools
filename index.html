Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 1, 2026

1. Introduction

KVR Smart Developer ("we", "our", or "us") is deeply committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy details how KVR Smart Tools handles data. Because our application is designed to be highly secure and offline-first, our data collection is strictly minimal to completely non-existent.

2. Data Collection & Usage

KVR Smart Tools operates locally on your device. We do not collect, transmit, distribute, sell, or store your personal data on external servers. Files processed via PDF tools, text converted, passwords generated, and calculations made remain entirely on your smartphone's local storage or RAM during runtime and are destroyed upon app closure.

3. Permissions Required

To function correctly, KVR Smart Tools may request certain device permissions. These are used strictly locally for the specific tool you are using:

  • Camera: Required only for the QR & Barcode Scanner tools. Images are processed locally and never saved or transmitted.
  • Storage / Media: Required to save generated PDFs, export text files, or read files you explicitly select for processing.
  • Network/Internet: Required strictly for specific tools (e.g., IP Lookup, Currency Converter fetching real-time exchange rates).

4. Third-Party Services & Advertising

KVR Smart Tools contains absolutely no third-party advertisements. We do not use advertising SDKs (like AdMob) or tracking SDKs that share your behavioral data with third parties. Your usage is your own business.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or suggestions regarding our Privacy Policy, please contact us directly at: venkatesh.official4775@gmail.com.
